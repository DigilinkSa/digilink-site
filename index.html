Company Overview

SAUDI-BASED INDUSTRIAL CONSULTING

DigiLink is a Saudi-based industrial consulting firm specializing in manufacturing performance, digital transformation, Industry 4.0 strategy, and Smart Industry Readiness Index (SIRI) assessments. With over 27 years of experience in manufacturing, maintenance, and reliability, DigiLink supports factories across the Kingdom in improving operational performance and aligning with Saudi Vision 2030 industrial objectives.

OUR VISION

To be the leading partner enabling Saudi factories to achieve world-class digital maturity and sustainable operational excellence.

OUR MISSION

To provide Saudi industries with reliable, data-driven, and globally benchmarked consulting services that accelerate their Industry 4.0 readiness and create measurable transformation impact.

Why DigiLink?

Certified SIRI Assessors

Our staff hold official Certified SIRI Assessor accreditation issued by INCIT and TÜV SÜD Middle East. This certification enables DigiLink to conduct official SIRI assessments recognized internationally and required by government programs.

Deep Local Expertise

With 27+ years in manufacturing, maintenance, and reliability, we deploy methodologies optimized for Saudi factories—especially small to mid-size plants (SMEs) seeking cost-effective maturity improvements.

End-to-End Support

We provide full Digital Transformation Support, from initial assessment to feasibility analysis, roadmap design, implementation support, and progress tracking.
