Company Overview

SAUDI-BASED INDUSTRIAL CONSULTING

DigiLink is a specialized Saudi industrial consulting firm focused on manufacturing performance, digitalization, AI readiness, automation planning, and Industry 4.0 strategy. With over 27 years of hands-on experience in manufacturing, maintenance, and reliability, we help factories improve productivity, reduce dependency on repetitive low-skill activities, strengthen data visibility, and build practical transformation roadmaps aligned with Saudi industrial competitiveness.

OUR VISION

To be a leading Saudi partner enabling factories to become smarter, more productive, AI-ready, and less dependent on repetitive manual work.

OUR MISSION

To provide Saudi industries with reliable, data-driven consulting services that convert digitalization, automation, AI readiness, and reliability improvement into measurable operational impact.

Future Factory Digitalization & AI Readiness

The objective is to build a smarter factory: higher digital maturity, practical AI readiness, automated repetitive work, better machine visibility, stronger reliability, and a workforce focused on supervision, analysis, and improvement rather than manual follow-up.

Digital Operations Visibility

Convert manual follow-up, paper records, and disconnected spreadsheets into reliable digital workflows, production dashboards, quality records, and real-time operational visibility.

AI Readiness Before AI Adoption

Assess whether strategy, data quality, system integration, governance, skills, and use cases are ready before investing in AI dashboards, predictive models, or advanced analytics.

Automation of Repetitive Work

Identify repetitive, low-skill, and error-prone activities that can be improved through automation, digital workflows, barcode/RFID, machine connectivity, or smart inspection.

Reliability & Downtime Reduction

Link transformation initiatives to asset performance, preventive maintenance discipline, downtime analysis, spare parts visibility, and stronger coordination between production and maintenance.

Workforce Upskilling

Shift the workforce from manual recording and reactive follow-up toward system-based execution, equipment monitoring, problem solving, data interpretation, and continuous improvement.

Practical Transformation Roadmap

Prioritize initiatives by operational value, readiness, complexity, cost, dependencies, and expected impact — avoiding technology investments that the factory is not ready to absorb.
